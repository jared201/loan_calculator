Loan Calculator Documentation

Learn how to use the loan calculator service.

Overview

The loan calculator helps you calculate the total cost of a loan based on the principal amount, add-on rate, tenor, and various fees.

Inputs

  • Principal: The initial amount of the loan.
  • Add-on Rate: The interest rate applied to the loan.
  • Tenor: The duration of the loan in months.
  • Documentary Stamp Fee: The fee for the documentary stamp.
  • Disbursement Fees: Any additional fees for disbursement.
  • Other Charges: Any other applicable charges.

Output

The calculator returns the total cost of the loan, including all fees and interest.

Example

For example, if you have a principal of $10,000, an add-on rate of 5%, a tenor of 12 months, a documentary stamp fee of $100, disbursement fees of $50, and other charges of $20, the calculator will provide the total cost of the loan.
